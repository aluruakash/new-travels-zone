It all began in 2011, with a small realization that began to grow into a dream. Our team of 2 travel professionals desired to refresh the passion for their work and love for travel that brought them into the industry in the first place. We craved a professional atmosphere with greater satisfaction to the clients, so we put our heads and experience together for a complete overhaul of the old corporate model. We wanted to change the way Indians look at travel, to open up new possibilities for our clients

With innovations in mind and extensive knowledge of the trade, we built TRAVELS ZONE in 2014 to serve the travelling public as we would want to be served. After all, love of travel is why we are in this business

We wanted our company to be recognized for its honesty, transparency, and reliability. We continue to strive toward these goals to provide memorable, relaxing holidays for our clients
